Badge List

Technique Badges

Power Strike

BR Cost: 1 BR

MP Cost: 2 MP

Effect: When making an attack roll, you can boost the attack to roll an additional die of damage. This declaration must be made before rolling to attack. Equipping multiple copies adds an additional die per badge, but raises the MP required by 2.


Power Strike

BR Cost: 1 BR

MP Cost: 0 MP

Effect: As a bonus action, you can make a History check on one enemy creature to discern their maximum HP, Armor Class, and base stats. Some additional, but vague information will be provided according to the tattled enemy in question. The DC to make a check is 10 + the CR rating of the tattled creature (CR ratings below 1 count as 0). Equipping multiple badges decreases the DC save by 2.
